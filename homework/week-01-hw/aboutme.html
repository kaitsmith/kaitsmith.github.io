Hi! I'm Kait.

When I'm not busy designing beautiful experiences, I restore old furniture, go on bike rides, collect vinyl and antique books, work on the herb garden on my fire escape, and hang out with my crazy cat Camille.


Though I earned an undergraduate degree in Graphic Design, I worked as a publicist for Moleskine, Internet Week New York, Rolling Stone Magazine, and Disney ABC before becoming a User Experience Designer.
